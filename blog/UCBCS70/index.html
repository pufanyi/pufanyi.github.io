1 Truth Tables

(a)

$P$ $Q$ $Q\lor P$ $P\land(Q\lor P)$ $P\land Q$
T T T T T

When $P=\mathtt{T},Q=\mathtt{F}$, $\mathrm{LHS}=\mathtt{T}$ but $\mathrm{RHS}=\mathtt{F}$.

So $\mathrm{LHS}\not\equiv \mathrm{RHS}$.
